## जवाफदेही (*https://jawafdehi.org*)

**Promoting Transparency & Accountability in Nepali Governance**

Damodar Dahal
December 23, 2022

---

**Jawafdehi** (जवाफदेही - "Accountability" in Nepali) is an open-source civic technology platform dedicated to promoting transparency and accountability in Nepali governance.

### Mission
Create a comprehensive, transparent, and accessible database of corruption cases in Nepal, empowering citizens with information and promoting accountability in governance.

---

## What We Do

- **📋 Document & Archive** - Systematically collect and digitize corruption cases to act as the country's long term memory against corruption.
- **🗣️ Narrow the Data Gap** - Free API and information about corruption cases for journalists, academics, corruption watchdogs, and other stakeholders
- **💪 Empower Citizens** - Allow reporting of corruption cases
- **📊 Track Progress** - Monitor case developments and legal proceedings

## Our Core Offering: AgniAI

AI-assisted data enrichment system that transforms raw documents into structured data through intelligent processing.

### Key Capabilities
- **📄 Source**: Government sources (CIAA, etc.), media (online newspaper and Facebook), and direct sources
- **🎯 Smart Matching**: Links to existing NES database from the sourced data
- **👥 Human-in-the-Loop**: Interactive review workflow for integrity
